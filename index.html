About Me

I am a communications professional, content creator, and MBA candidate at Texas Tech University's Rawls College of Business, where I maintain a 3.8 GPA. My work lives at the intersection of storytelling, strategy, and execution, combining creative media production with operational leadership and audience engagement.

My professional background spans broadcast television, higher education, and digital media. At Ramar Communications, I supervised master control operations for 15+ local television channels, managing advertising content, live sports, breaking news, and high-pressure broadcast environments. At Health.edu within Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center, I’ve spent nearly a decade producing medical continuing education content, supporting large-scale learning initiatives, and managing relationships with more than 150 partner organizations nationwide.

I also serve as Social Media & Communications Coordinator for The Broadway Shuffle, a Texas Tech sports podcast, where I lead weekly multi-platform content strategy, episode editing, graphic design, and audience-focused digital storytelling.
